    In this prototype software selected entities of an Ontology for Biomedical Investigations (OBI) conformant ontology are presented in HTML forms.  Here GenEpiO datums and data collection entities are shown. A term's preferred name, definition, synonyms and database cross-reference fields are shown directly or by mousing over a field label or its menu indicator.
